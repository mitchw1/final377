About BaseStat Pro

BaseStat Pro is a full-stack baseball analytics application designed by students in the INST 377 course. Our goal is to combine real-time baseball statistics with user-friendly fantasy tools and visual insights for all baseball fans and data enthusiasts.

We integrate the MLB-StatsAPI to power our live data, and use Supabase for storing custom user submissions. Our front-end is built with vanilla HTML/CSS/JS, enhanced with Chart.js for visualizations and Swiper.js for an interactive experience.

This project demonstrates our skills in RESTful API integration, asynchronous JavaScript, backend database operations, and responsive design.

Whether you're a seasoned fantasy player or just love MLB stats, BaseStat Pro gives you the tools to stay informed and ahead of the game.
